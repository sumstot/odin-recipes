No Knead Focaccia

About the dish

This super easy no knead focaccia recipe will shock your friends. With only a few ingredients, it's easy to make and tastes fantastic!

Ingredients

  • 400g of flour
  • 250ml of water
  • 10g of yeast
  • 30ml of high quality olive oil
  • Rosemary
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix yeast with 250ml of warm water. Add a tablespoon of flour. Let sit for 10 minutes
  2. Once your yeast has foamed, mix in with flour. Stir until combined. Add a little olive oil to your hands to help with sticky dough
  3. Let dough sit for 30 minutes
  4. After 30 minutes, raise and fold the dough on itself four times, turning 90° each time
  5. Let rest for 30 minutes
  6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 three more times.
  7. Once the dough is ready, transfer to a well oiled baking tray
  8. Pour 30ml of olive oil on top. Stick fingers into the dough to create dimples
  9. Add rosemary
  10. Bake at 400° for 30 minutes or until golden brown
  11. Once finished, transfer to a wired rack, salt the focaccia and let cool
